Real World Application

Weekly Operating Workflow

  1. Export the newest Etsy or Shopify order data.
  2. Update config/current_inventory_template.csv with real inventory and open purchase orders.
  3. Run python run_project.py.
  4. Review category reorder signals before placing supplier orders.
  5. Use style allocation to split category buys into specific variants.

Business Decisions Supported

  • Which product families are likely to stock out during supplier lead time.
  • How much to reorder by category instead of reacting to noisy style-level sales.
  • Which styles should receive more or less of the category purchase quantity.
  • Whether historical demand is transferring to the restarted shop.
Production note: the forecast becomes operationally useful when current inventory, on-order units, supplier lead times, and product costs are connected to live Cronk systems.
